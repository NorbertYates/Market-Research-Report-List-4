<p><p>Shelf liners come in two main types: meshed and smooth. Meshed shelf liners are designed with a grid-like pattern that allows for ventilation and easy cleaning. These liners are ideal for use in areas where moisture and mildew are a concern, as they provide efficient air circulation. On the other hand, smooth shelf liners offer a sleek and flat surface that is easy to wipe clean. They are commonly used in areas where a more polished and refined look is desired.</p></p>
