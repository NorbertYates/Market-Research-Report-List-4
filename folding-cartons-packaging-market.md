<p><p>Folding cartons packaging market consists of two main types: Standard cartons and Aseptic cartons. They are typically made of paperboard and are easily customizable in terms of size, shape, and design. Aseptic cartons, on the other hand, are specifically designed for perishable goods that require extended shelf life without refrigeration. They are usually made of multiple layers of materials to ensure product safety and preservation.</p></p>
