<p><ul><li>TEOM Monitor</li><li>Beta Attenuation Monitor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PM2.5 air quality detectors come in various types, with TEOM monitors and Beta Attenuation monitors being the most common. TEOM monitors use a heated element to measure particle concentrations in the air, while Beta Attenuation monitors use a sensor that measures the amount of light passing through a filter. Other types of PM2.5 detectors include laser particle counters, optical particle counters, and diffusion chargers. Each type of monitor has its own advantages and limitations in terms of accuracy, cost, and ease of use.</p></p>
