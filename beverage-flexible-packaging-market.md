<p><p>The beverage flexible packaging market includes various types of materials used for packaging such as PE (Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), PP (Polypropylene), PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), and PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride). PE and LDPE are commonly used for their durability and flexibility, while PP is known for its heat resistance and strength. Each material offers unique advantages for packaging beverages to meet different market demands.</p></p>
