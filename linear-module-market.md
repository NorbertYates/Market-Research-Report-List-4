<p><ul><li>Ball Screw Linear Module</li><li>Synchronous Belt Linear Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear modules are devices used in various industries for precise linear motion. There are two main types of linear modules: Ball Screw Linear Module and Synchronous Belt Linear Module. The Ball Screw Linear Module utilizes a rotating ball screw to convert rotary motion into linear motion, providing high accuracy and efficiency. On the other hand, the Synchronous Belt Linear Module uses a toothed belt to transfer motion, offering cost-effective and quieter operation. Both types of linear modules cater to different applications and requirements in the market.</p></p>
