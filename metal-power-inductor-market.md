<p><p>Metal power inductors are widely used in various electronic devices to store and release energy in the form of magnetic fields. There are two main types of metal power inductors in the market - thin film inductors and wire wound inductors. Thin film inductors are made by depositing a thin layer of metal on a substrate, resulting in compact and high-performance components. On the other hand, wire wound inductors use a wire coil to create the magnetic field, providing high inductance values and better power handling capabilities.</p></p>
