<p><p>A flow battery is a type of rechargeable battery that employs two liquids (electrolytes) as active material. These liquids are stored in external tanks and are pumped through a cell or stack where they are electrochemically transformed, producing electricity. This unique design allows flow batteries to scale up in capacity more easily compared to traditional batteries.</p><p>The flow battery market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 19.80% during the forecast period.
