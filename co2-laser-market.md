<p><p>A CO2 laser is a gas laser that uses carbon dioxide as the active medium to emit a coherent beam of light in the infrared spectrum. These lasers are widely used in various industries for cutting, engraving, welding, marking, and drilling of materials such as metals, plastics, ceramics, and wood. The high precision and efficiency of CO2 lasers make them popular in the manufacturing sector.</p><p>The CO2 Laser Market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for high-quality laser cutting and engraving solutions across industries such as automotive, aerospace, healthcare, and electronics. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period. Technological advancements in CO2 laser systems, such as integration with automation and robotics, enhanced processing speed, and improved energy efficiency, are driving the market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the CO2 Laser Market include the adoption of fiber-coupled CO2 lasers for higher flexibility and functionality, the development of compact and portable CO2 laser systems for small-scale applications, and the growing popularity of multi-axis laser processing for complex and intricate designs. With the increasing emphasis on precision and productivity in manufacturing processes, the CO2 Laser Market is expected to witness continued growth in the coming years.</p></p>
