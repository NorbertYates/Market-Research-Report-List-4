<p><p>Double Block and Bleed (DBB) Valves are essential for the oil and gas industry to ensure safety and prevent leaks in pipelines. Single DBB Valves provide a single block valve and two isolation valves in a single compact unit, while Double DBB Valves offer two block valves and one bleed valve in a single unit. Single DBB Valves are commonly used in low-pressure applications, while Double DBB Valves are preferred for high-pressure environments. Both types provide a cost-effective solution for isolation and testing in oil and gas operations.</p></p>
