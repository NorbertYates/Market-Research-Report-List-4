<p><p>Concrete block making machines are classified into three main types based on their level of automation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully-automatic. Manual machines require the operator to manually input materials and operate the machine. Semi-automatic machines have some automated functions, such as block formation and ejection, but still require manual input for other processes. Fully-automatic machines are completely automated, requiring minimal human intervention as they can handle the entire block-making process from material input to block formation and ejection.</p></p>
