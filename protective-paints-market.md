<p><strong>The Protective Paints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne Coatings</li><li>Water-borne Coatings</li><li>Powder Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protective paints market is categorized into three main types based on the composition of the coating: Solvent-borne coatings, Water-borne coatings, and Powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents as the carrier for the protective paint, while water-borne coatings use water as the carrier. Powder coatings are applied in a dry powder form and are cured using heat. Each type of coating offers unique advantages in terms of performance, durability, and environmental impact, making them suitable for various applications in the protective paints market.</p></p>
