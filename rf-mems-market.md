RF capacitance and inductance devices focus on adjusting the electrical characteristics of RF circuits. RF switches and derivatives enable efficient routing of RF signals. RF filters help to enhance the quality of RF transmissions by selectively allowing certain frequencies to pass through. Other types in the market include components such as resonators, phase shifters, and tunable filters for diverse RF applications.</p></p>
