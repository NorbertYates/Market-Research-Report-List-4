<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal CNC milling equipment is characterized by the spindle being parallel to the worktable, allowing for more efficient and accurate machining of large and heavy workpieces. Vertical CNC milling equipment, on the other hand, has the spindle perpendicular to the worktable, making it ideal for complex shapes and intricate designs. Both types of machines cater to different applications and industries, with horizontal machines being popular in industries such as aerospace and automotive, while vertical machines are commonly used in the production of electronic components and medical devices.</p></p>
