<p><p>Bone conduction headphones are a type of headphones that deliver audio through the bones of the skull rather than through the ear canal. These headphones work by vibrating the bones in the user's skull, which in turn, transmits sound directly to the inner ear. This allows the user to still hear ambient noise while listening to music or taking calls, making them popular among athletes, cyclists, and those with hearing impairments.</p><p>The bone conduction headphones market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.40% during the forecast period.
