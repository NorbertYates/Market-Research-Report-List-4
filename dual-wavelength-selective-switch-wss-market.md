<p><ul><li>LCOS Based</li><li>MEMS Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) market can be categorized into two types based on technology - LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) Based and MEMS (Micro-electromechanical Systems) Based. LCOS Based WSS offers high performance and precise wavelength selection through liquid crystal technology. On the other hand, MEMS Based WSS provides fast switching speeds and compact size through micro-mechanical systems. Both types cater to the growing demand for flexible and efficient optical networks in telecommunications and data centers.</p></p>
