<p><p>The laptop display panels market consists of two main types: LCD and OLED. LCD panels use liquid crystals to produce images and are known for their cost-effectiveness and energy efficiency. On the other hand, OLED panels utilize organic light-emitting diodes to create images, offering superior contrast, color reproduction, and flexibility. While LCD panels are more commonly found in laptops due to their affordability, OLED panels are gaining popularity for their higher quality display features.</p></p>
