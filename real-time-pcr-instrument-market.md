<p><p>Real-time PCR instruments are designed for amplification and quantification of DNA in real-time. The market can be segmented into two main types: One-step Real Time PCR and Two-step Real Time PCR. One-step PCR combines reverse transcription and PCR in a single tube, simplifying the process and reducing the risk of contamination. Two-step PCR involves separate reverse transcription and PCR steps, allowing for greater control over the reaction conditions. Both types are widely used in research, diagnostics, and other applications requiring sensitive and accurate DNA quantification.</p></p>
