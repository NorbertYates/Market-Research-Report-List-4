<p><ul><li>Distributed Raman Optical Amplifier</li><li>Lumped Raman Optical Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Raman Optical Amplifiers are used in fiber-optic communication systems to enhance the signal strength. There are two main types of Raman Optical Amplifiers: Distributed Raman Optical Amplifiers and Lumped Raman Optical Amplifiers. Distributed Raman Optical Amplifiers are distributed along the length of the fiber, providing continuous amplification. On the other hand, Lumped Raman Optical Amplifiers use discrete amplification points along the fiber line. Both types have their advantages and are used in different applications based on the specific requirements of the communication system.</p></p>
