<p><strong>The Handgun Red Dot Sights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SFP</li><li>FFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handgun Red Dot Sights market types can be categorized into two main categories: Second Focal Plane (SFP) and First Focal Plane (FFP). SFP sights have the reticle located in the rear of the scope, which means the reticle size remains constant regardless of magnification level. In contrast, FFP sights have the reticle located in front of the magnification lens, meaning the reticle size changes relative to the target as the magnification level is adjusted. Both SFP and FFP sights have their own advantages and are chosen based on individual preferences and shooting requirements.</p></p>
