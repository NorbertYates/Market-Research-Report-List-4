<p><p>Asthma devices market comprises two main types: inhalers and nebulizers. Inhalers are handheld devices that deliver medication directly into the lungs through a fine mist or spray, allowing for quick relief of asthma symptoms. Nebulizers, on the other hand, are larger machines that convert liquid medication into a fine mist for inhalation through a mask or mouthpiece, making them more suitable for individuals who have difficulty using handheld inhalers. Both devices are essential in managing asthma symptoms and improving quality of life for asthma patients.</p></p>
