<p><p>The Conveyor Transfer Robot market includes two main types: Roller and Belt systems. Roller systems use a series of cylindrical rollers to move objects along a path, providing a smooth and efficient transfer process. On the other hand, Belt systems utilize a continuous loop of material to transport items, offering versatility and durability. Both Roller and Belt systems are essential components in various industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and transportation, providing reliable and automated solutions for handling materials.</p></p>
