<p><p>The fiber optic products market can be divided into four main types: wafers & chips, cables, components, and modules. Wafers & chips refer to the semiconductor materials used in fiber optic technology. Cables are the physical medium through which signals are transmitted. Components are the individual parts that make up the fiber optic system, such as connectors and transmitters. Modules are preassembled units that perform specific functions within the fiber optic network. Each of these types play a crucial role in the overall functionality of fiber optic systems.</p></p>
