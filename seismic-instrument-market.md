<p><strong>The Seismic Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Period Seismograph</li><li>Long Period Seismograph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Short period seismographs are instruments that are designed to detect high-frequency seismic waves, typically associated with earthquakes, explosions, or other sudden sources of ground motion. They are used to monitor and record short-term seismic activity. Long period seismographs, on the other hand, are used to detect low-frequency seismic waves, which are associated with volcanic activity or oceanic disturbances. They are designed to record long-term changes in ground motion and are used for monitoring tectonic movements and studying seismic events over extended periods of time.</p></p>
