<p><p>Automatic wafer loading machines can be categorized into two main types: inline and offline. Inline machines are integrated into the production line and load wafers directly from one process to the next, ensuring a continuous flow of production. On the other hand, offline machines operate independently from the production line and require manual intervention to load and unload wafers. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs and requirements of the manufacturing process.</p></p>
