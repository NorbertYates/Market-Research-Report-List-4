<p><strong>The Redundancy Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DPST</li><li>DPDT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Redundancy switches in the market come in various types, with DPST (Double Pole, Single Throw) and DPDT (Double Pole, Double Throw) being two common configurations. DPST switches provide control over two separate circuits with a single toggle, while DPDT switches offer control over two circuits and can divert between two different connections. Both types are widely used in applications where backup or redundant systems are necessary for maintaining a continuous and reliable operation.</p></p>
