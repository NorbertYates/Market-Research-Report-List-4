<p><strong>The Photoresist Anti-reflective Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Anti-reflective Coatings</li><li>Top Anti-reflective Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photoresist anti-reflective coatings are used in the semiconductor and electronics industry to reduce reflection during photolithography processes. Bottom anti-reflective coatings are applied directly on the substrate to prevent reflections from the underlying material. On the other hand, top anti-reflective coatings are applied on top of the photoresist to reduce reflections from the ambient light. Both types of coatings help improve the accuracy and resolution of the patterning process in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
