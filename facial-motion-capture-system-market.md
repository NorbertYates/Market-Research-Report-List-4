<p><p>Facial Motion Capture Systems can be categorized into three main types based on the technology they use: Magnetic Motion Capture, Mechanical Motion Capture, and Optical Motion Capture. </p><p>Magnetic Motion Capture systems use sensors to track movement by measuring changes in magnetic fields. Mechanical Motion Capture systems use physical markers attached to the face to track movements. Optical Motion Capture systems use cameras and specialized software to track facial movements. Each of these types offers a unique set of advantages and limitations for capturing and analyzing facial expressions and gestures.</p></p>
