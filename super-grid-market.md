The High Voltage Grid operates at voltages above 100 kV, the EHV Grid operates at voltages above 345 kV, and the UHV Grid operates at voltages above 800 kV. They play a crucial role in integrating renewable energy sources, improving grid reliability, and meeting the increasing demand for electricity.</p></p>
