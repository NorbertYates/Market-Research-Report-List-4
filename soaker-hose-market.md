<p><p>Soaker hoses are popular irrigation tools used in gardening and landscaping. There are two main types of soaker hoses available in the market: flat soaker hoses and round soaker hoses. Flat soaker hoses are designed to lay flat on the ground, allowing water to seep out evenly along its length. Round soaker hoses, on the other hand, are cylindrical in shape and provide a more concentrated watering pattern. Both types of hoses are efficient and effective in delivering water directly to the roots of plants, promoting healthy growth and minimizing water waste.</p></p>
