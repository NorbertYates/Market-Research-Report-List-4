<p><ul><li>Front Silver Paste</li><li>Back Silver Paste</li><li>Aluminum Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar cell electrode paste is used in the manufacturing of solar cells to create conductivity and adhesion between the semiconductor material and the metal contacts. There are three main types of electrode paste in the market - Front Silver Paste, Back Silver Paste, and Aluminum Paste. Front Silver Paste is applied to the front side of the solar cell to create a positive electrode, while Back Silver Paste is used on the backside for the negative electrode. Aluminum Paste is also used in certain solar cell designs as an alternative to silver paste.</p></p>
