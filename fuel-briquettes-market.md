<p><p>Fuel briquettes come in various types such as solid cylindrical type, RUF type, and Pini-Kay type. </p><p>Solid cylindrical type briquettes are compact and uniform in shape, making them easy to handle and store. RUF type briquettes are rectangular in shape and have a high energy density, which results in efficient combustion. Pini-Kay type briquettes are dense and have a hole through the center, allowing for better airflow and longer burn time.</p><p>Each type of briquette offers distinct advantages and can cater to different preferences and needs in the fuel briquettes market.</p></p>
