<p><p>Log grapples can be divided into two main types - hydraulic log grapples and mechanical log grapples. Hydraulic log grapples utilize hydraulic power to open and close the grapple arms, providing a strong and efficient grip on logs. On the other hand, mechanical log grapples rely on manual controls and mechanical mechanisms to operate. Both types of log grapples are essential in forestry and logging operations for efficiently lifting, moving, and loading logs onto trucks or processing equipment.</p></p>
