<p><ul><li>Winding Hoists</li><li>Friction Hoists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mine hoists are commonly categorized into two main types: winding hoists and friction hoists. Winding hoists are designed to lift and lower loads using a drum or sheave, typically powered by an electric motor. Friction hoists, on the other hand, operate by applying friction between the hoist ropes and the drum or sheave to lift or lower loads. Both types of hoists play a critical role in underground mining operations by providing efficient and reliable means of transporting materials and personnel.</p></p>
