<p><p>Whole house water filtration systems come in three main types: Single Stage, Dual Stage, and Triple Stage. Single Stage systems typically use one filter to remove sediments and larger particles. Dual Stage systems use two filters to remove a wider range of contaminants, such as chlorine and heavy metals. Triple Stage systems offer the most comprehensive filtration by including an additional filter for specific contaminants like chemicals, pesticides, and pharmaceuticals. Each type of system offers varying levels of protection for ensuring clean and safe water throughout the home.</p></p>
