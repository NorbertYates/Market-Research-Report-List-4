<p><strong>The Marine Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Bearing</li><li>Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine bearing market consists of two main types: rolling bearing and sliding bearing. Rolling bearings use rolling elements such as balls or rollers to reduce friction between moving parts, providing higher efficiency and lower maintenance compared to sliding bearings. Sliding bearings, on the other hand, rely on a sliding motion between surfaces to reduce friction. While sliding bearings may have higher initial costs and require more maintenance, they are preferred in applications with heavy loads and low speeds. Both types play a crucial role in the marine industry.</p></p>
