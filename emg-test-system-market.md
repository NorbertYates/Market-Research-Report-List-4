<p><ul><li>Dual Channel Testing</li><li>Multi Channel Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EMG Test System market includes Dual Channel Testing and Multi Channel Testing types. Dual Channel Testing involves the use of two electrodes to measure the electrical activity of a specific muscle or muscle group. In contrast, Multi Channel Testing utilizes multiple electrodes to assess the activity of multiple muscles simultaneously. Both types of systems are widely used in neurophysiology and rehabilitation settings to assess muscle function, diagnose neuromuscular disorders, and monitor the progress of treatment.</p></p>
