<p><p>Adjustable attenuators are devices used to reduce the amplitude of a signal without distorting its waveform. Manual adjustable attenuators require physical adjustment by the user, providing flexibility in controlling signal strength. Electric adjustable attenuators utilize electronic control to adjust signal attenuation, offering precise and remote control capabilities. Both types cater to different user needs, with manual attenuators being more cost-effective and electric attenuators offering greater precision and convenience. The market for these attenuators continues to grow with the increasing demand for customizable signal strength adjustment in various industries.</p></p>
