<p><p>The Semiconductor Wafer Polishing Pad market is divided into two main types: Hard CMP Pads and Soft CMP Pads. Hard CMP Pads are designed for applications requiring high material removal rates and are more durable, making them suitable for rough polishing processes. On the other hand, Soft CMP Pads are ideal for applications that require precise polishing and smoother surface finishes. They are less aggressive but provide better control over the polishing process, making them suitable for delicate materials or intricate designs.</p></p>
