<p><p>Changeable Message Signs (CMS) have two main market types: Fixed Type and Mobile Type. Fixed Type CMS are permanently installed at a specific location, such as highways or busy intersections. They provide information about traffic conditions, construction, or emergencies. Mobile Type CMS can be easily transported and temporarily set up at different locations as needed. They are commonly used for events, construction zones, or other temporary situations where information needs to be communicated to drivers. Both types of CMS serve the purpose of conveying important messages to motorists.</p></p>
