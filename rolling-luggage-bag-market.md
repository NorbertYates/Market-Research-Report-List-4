<p><p>Rolling luggage bags come in two main types: 4-wheel and 2-wheel designs. 4-wheel bags, also known as spinner luggage, have wheels that can rotate 360 degrees, making them easy to maneuver in any direction. They are ideal for navigating crowded airports or busy streets. 2-wheel bags, on the other hand, are more traditional and are pulled behind the traveler. They are typically more stable on uneven terrain and easier to control on inclines. Both types offer convenience and ease of transport for travelers on the go.</p></p>
