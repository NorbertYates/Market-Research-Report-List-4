<p><ul><li>Energy Saving Thermal Insulation Coatings</li><li>Functional Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Architectural Glass Coatings can be categorized into two major types: Energy Saving Thermal Insulation Coatings and Functional Coatings. Energy Saving Thermal Insulation Coatings are designed to reduce heat transfer through the glass, thus improving energy efficiency and lowering heating and cooling costs. On the other hand, Functional Coatings offer additional benefits such as self-cleaning, anti-glare, anti-bacterial, and anti-fog properties, making the glass more versatile and easier to maintain. Both types of coatings play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and longevity of architectural glass applications.</p></p>
