<p><p>Repair mortars are materials used to restore and repair damaged concrete structures. The two main types are polymer-modified cementitious mortar and epoxy-based mortar. Polymer-modified cementitious mortar is a blend of Portland cement, aggregate, and polymer additives that enhance bonding strength and flexibility. Epoxy-based mortar is made of epoxy resins and hardeners, providing high strength and chemical resistance. Both types offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for a wide range of repair applications in construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
