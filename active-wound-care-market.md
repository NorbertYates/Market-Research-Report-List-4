<p><p>Active wound care market includes dressings and grafts. Dressings are used to cover and protect wounds, absorbing excess moisture and promoting healing. They can be made of various materials like foam, hydrocolloid, alginate, and collagen. Grafts are used for large or chronic wounds, providing a temporary or permanent barrier while promoting tissue regeneration. They can be synthetic or biological, like skin or amniotic tissue. Both dressings and grafts play a crucial role in wound management and healing.</p></p>
