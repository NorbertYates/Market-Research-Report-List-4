<p><p>Airport transceivers are communication devices used in airports to facilitate communication between air traffic controllers and pilots. There are three main types of airport transceivers: UHF (ultra high frequency), VHF (very high frequency), and HF (high frequency). UHF transceivers are used for short-range communications within the airport, VHF transceivers are used for medium-range communications within the airport and between the airport and aircraft, while HF transceivers are used for long-range communications between the airport and aircraft over large distances.</p></p>
