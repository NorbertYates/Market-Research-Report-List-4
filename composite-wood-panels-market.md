<p><p>Composite wood panels consist of various types such as engineered wood panels, hardboard, particleboard, and MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard). Engineered wood panels are made by bonding together wood strands, veneers, or particles with adhesives. Hardboard is a type of engineered wood product made from compressed wood fibers. Particleboard is composed of wood particles bonded together with resin. MDF is made from fine wood fibers bonded with resin under high temperature and pressure. These different types cater to different applications and markets within the composite wood panels industry.</p></p>
