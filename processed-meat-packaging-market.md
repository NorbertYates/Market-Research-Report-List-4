<p><strong>The Processed Meat Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP)</li><li>Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP)</li><li>Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Processed meat packaging market types include Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP), which involves altering the atmosphere inside the packaging to extend the shelf life of the product. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) tightly wraps the meat in a plastic film using a vacuum to reduce oxygen levels, slowing down bacterial growth. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) molds the packaging around the meat, creating a tight seal to maintain freshness. Other packaging methods may include shrink wrapping or resealable pouches.</p></p>
