<p><ul><li>Cured-in-place</li><li>Pull-in-place</li><li>Pipe Bursting</li><li>Internal Pipe Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipe relining is a method used for repairing damaged pipes without the need for excavation. There are various types of pipe relining methods available in the market: cured-in-place relining, pull-in-place relining, pipe bursting, and internal pipe coating. Cured-in-place relining involves inserting a liner into the pipe and curing it in place. Pull-in-place relining involves pulling a liner into the pipe using a special machine. Pipe bursting involves breaking the old pipe and replacing it with a new one. Internal pipe coating involves applying a coating to the inner surface of the pipe to repair damage.</p></p>
