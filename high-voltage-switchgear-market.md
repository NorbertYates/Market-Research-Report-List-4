<p><strong>The High Voltage Switchgear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS)</li><li>Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Voltage Switchgear refers to equipment used to control electrical power in distribution networks. Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS) uses sulfur hexafluoride gas to insulate and protect the components inside, allowing for more compact design and higher reliability. Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS) uses air as the insulating medium, making it more cost-effective but requiring larger space. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the safe distribution of electricity in power systems and are widely used in industries and utilities worldwide.</p></p>
