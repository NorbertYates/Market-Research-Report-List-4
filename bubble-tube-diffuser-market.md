<p><p>Bubble tube diffusers are categorized into two main types based on bubble size: Fine Bubble Membrane Diffusers and Coarse Bubble Membrane Diffusers. Fine bubble diffusers produce small bubbles, increasing the oxygen transfer efficiency and providing a larger contact area for mixing and aeration. Coarse bubble diffusers, on the other hand, produce larger bubbles which are suitable for mixing and agitation in wastewater treatment applications. Both types play a crucial role in improving the efficiency and effectiveness of aeration systems in water treatment processes.</p></p>
