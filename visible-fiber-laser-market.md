<p><p>The Visible Fiber Laser market consists of two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) Type and Pulsed Type. The Continuous Wave Type is characterized by a continuous output of laser beams, making it suitable for applications that require steady and consistent laser power. On the other hand, the Pulsed Type produces laser beams in short bursts or pulses, which is ideal for tasks that require high peak powers or precise control over pulse durations. Both types cater to specific market needs and applications in different industries.</p></p>
