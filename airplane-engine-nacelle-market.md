<p><strong>The Airplane Engine Nacelle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wing Mounted</li><li>Rear Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airplane engine nacelles are classified into two main types: Wing Mounted and Rear Mounted. Wing Mounted nacelles are attached underneath the wings of the aircraft, where the engine is mounted within the structure of the wing itself. This design helps to reduce drag and increase aerodynamic efficiency. Rear Mounted nacelles, on the other hand, are located at the rear of the aircraft fuselage, behind the wings. This design is typically seen in smaller aircraft and provides easier access for maintenance and servicing.</p></p>
