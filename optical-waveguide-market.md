<p><p>Optical Waveguide market can be categorized into two main types: Channel Waveguide and Planar Waveguide. Channel Waveguide consists of a tiny channel where light propagates, typically used in fiber optics. On the other hand, Planar Waveguide is a flat, thin structure that guides light along its surface, commonly found in integrated optical circuits. Both types of waveguides play crucial roles in optical communication systems, optical sensors, and other applications where guiding and manipulating light is necessary.</p></p>
