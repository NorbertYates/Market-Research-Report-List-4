<p><p>Diamond abrasive blades are used for cutting hard materials like concrete, stone, and ceramics. There are three main types of diamond abrasive blades on the market: sintering, high-frequency welding, and laser welding. Sintering involves bonding diamond particles with metal under high pressure and temperature. High-frequency welding uses a high-frequency current to bond the diamond segments to the blade. Laser welding uses a laser beam to melt and bond the diamond segments to the blade. Each of these methods offers unique benefits in terms of durability and cutting efficiency.</p></p>
