<p><p>IBS Treatment Market consists of three main types based on the predominant bowel habits of patients. IBS-D refers to diarrhea-predominant IBS, characterized by frequent loose stools. IBS-C refers to constipation-predominant IBS, marked by infrequent stools and difficulty passing stool. IBS-M, on the other hand, refers to mixed IBS, where patients experience both diarrhea and constipation. Each type may require different approaches to treatment, such as lifestyle changes, dietary modifications, medications, and psychological therapies.</p></p>
