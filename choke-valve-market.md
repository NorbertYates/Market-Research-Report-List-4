<p><p>Choke valves are widely used in various industries for controlling the flow of fluids. There are two main types of choke valve markets - the straight-through type and angle type. Straight-through type choke valves have a linear flow path, allowing for smoother flow control. On the other hand, angle type choke valves have a bent flow path, which can provide better control over turbulent flow conditions. Both types have their own advantages and applications, depending on the specific requirements of the industry.</p></p>
