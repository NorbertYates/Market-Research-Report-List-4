<p><p>FPD Transfer Robots are used in the semiconductor industry to move large glass substrates during the production process. There are two main types of FPD Transfer Robots in the market: Atmospheric Robots and Vacuum Robots. Atmospheric Robots use grippers to pick up and move the substrates, while Vacuum Robots use suction cups to securely hold the substrates in place. Both types of robots offer precise and efficient handling of FPDs, contributing to the overall productivity and quality of the manufacturing process.</p></p>
