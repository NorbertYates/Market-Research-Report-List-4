<p><p>Stud finders are tools used to locate metal or wood studs behind walls before hanging objects like shelves or pictures. Magnetic stud finders work by detecting metal fasteners in studs, while electronic stud finders use sensors to identify changes in density within the wall. The magnetic stud finder is simple and inexpensive, but may not be as accurate as electronic stud finders. Electronic stud finders offer more precision and can detect both wood and metal studs, making them a popular choice among professionals and DIY enthusiasts.</p></p>
