<p><p>Wafer Level Packaging (WLP) market includes various types such as 3D TSV WLP, 2.5D TSV WLP, WLCSP, Nano WLP, and others like 2D TSV WLP and Compliant WLP. 3D TSV WLP involves stacking multiple dies vertically using Through-Silicon-Vias (TSVs), while 2.5D TSV WLP uses interposers for die stacking. WLCSP is a chip-scale packaging technology. Nano WLP involves miniaturizing packaging size. Other types like 2D TSV WLP use traditional TSV technology, and Compliant WLP offers flexibility in handling thermal expansion.</p></p>
