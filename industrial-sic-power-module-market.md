<p><p>Hybrid SiC modules combine silicon and SiC components to achieve higher efficiency and power density than traditional silicon modules. Full SiC modules use only SiC components, offering superior performance in terms of switching speed, power loss, and thermal management. Both types are increasingly adopted in various industrial applications such as power supplies, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems due to their ability to handle higher voltages and temperatures, leading to improved overall system efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
