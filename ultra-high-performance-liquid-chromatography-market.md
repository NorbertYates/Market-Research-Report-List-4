<p><p>Ultra High Performance Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) market offers various types of chromatography techniques including Ion Exchange Chromatography, Ion Exclusion Chromatography, and Ion Pair Chromatography. Ion Exchange Chromatography separates ions based on their charge, Ion Exclusion Chromatography excludes ions based on their size, and Ion Pair Chromatography uses ion pairing agents to improve the retention and separation of ions. Each of these techniques plays a crucial role in UHPLC analysis by providing different mechanisms for separating and identifying ions in complex samples.</p></p>
