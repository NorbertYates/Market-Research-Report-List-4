<p><p>Networking cables market can be categorized into three main types: coaxial cable, twisted pair, and optical fiber cable. Coaxial cable is known for its high resistance to interference, making it ideal for TV and internet connections. Twisted pair cables are commonly used for Ethernet connections due to their affordability and flexibility. Optical fiber cables, on the other hand, offer high-speed data transmission and are mostly used in long-distance networking applications. Each type of cable caters to different networking needs and preferences.</p></p>
