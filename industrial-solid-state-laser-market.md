<p><p>Industrial Solid State Laser market can be categorized into two types: Pulse Type and Continuous Type. Pulse Type lasers emit short bursts of energy with high peak power, making them suitable for applications such as precision cutting and drilling. Continuous Type lasers, on the other hand, emit a continuous beam of energy, making them ideal for applications like welding and cladding. Both types of lasers offer unique advantages and are used in a variety of industrial processes for their specific capabilities.</p></p>
