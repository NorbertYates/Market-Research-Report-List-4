<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home Audio Crossovers are devices used in audio systems to separate different frequencies and direct them to the appropriate speakers. There are two main types of markets for Home Audio Crossovers: Active and Passive. </p><p>Active crossovers use electronic circuits to split the audio signal before amplification, providing greater control and customization over the sound output. Passive crossovers, on the other hand, use passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors to split the signal after amplification. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different audio setups and preferences.</p></p>
