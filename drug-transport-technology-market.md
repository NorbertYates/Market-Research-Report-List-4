<p><p>Drug Transport Technology market can be categorized into different types based on the method of delivery. Oral drug transport technology involves delivering drugs through the mouth, typically in the form of tablets or capsules. Injectable drug transport technology involves delivering drugs through injections, either intravenously or intramuscularly. Topical drug transport technology involves delivering drugs through the skin, usually in the form of creams or patches. Implantable drug transport technology involves delivering drugs through surgically implanted devices or implants placed under the skin.</p></p>
