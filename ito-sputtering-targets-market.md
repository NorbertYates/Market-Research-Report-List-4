<p><strong>The ITO Sputtering Targets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ITO Sputtering Targets are commonly used in thin film deposition processes for various applications. Plane Targets are stationary and provide a uniform coating on the substrate, making them suitable for large-scale production. Rotating Targets, on the other hand, offer higher material utilization and better film quality by constantly exposing fresh material to the sputter source. Both types cater to different market demands, with Plane Targets being preferred for cost-effective production and Rotating Targets for high-quality, advanced applications.</p></p>
