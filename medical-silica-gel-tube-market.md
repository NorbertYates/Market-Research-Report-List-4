<p><ul><li>Extrusion Method</li><li>Mold Vulcanization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical silica gel tubes are commonly produced using two main methods: extrusion and mold vulcanization. </p><p>Extrusion method involves pushing the silica gel mixture through a die to create the desired shape of the tube. This method is efficient for mass production and allows for precise control of the tube's dimensions.</p><p>On the other hand, mold vulcanization involves curing the silica gel in a mold at high temperatures and pressures. This method is preferred for producing complex shapes and customized designs of medical silica gel tubes.</p></p>
