<p><p>Electrophotographic printing is widely used in various market types including offset, gravure, flexography, screen, and letterpress. Offset printing involves transferring an image from a plate to a rubber blanket and then onto a printing surface. Gravure printing uses an etched plate to produce high-quality images. Flexography utilizes flexible relief plates for printing on various substrates. Screen printing involves pushing ink through a mesh stencil onto a substrate. Letterpress printing involves pressing a raised surface onto paper to create an image.</p></p>
