<p><p>Titanium ore market consists of three main types: Ilmenite, Titanium Slag, and Rutile. Ilmenite is the most abundant titanium ore, known for its iron content. Titanium Slag is a byproduct of the production of titanium dioxide pigment and is used in the manufacturing of titanium metal. Rutile is the purest form of titanium ore and is prized for its high titanium dioxide content. Each type of ore serves different purposes within the titanium industry, catering to various needs and applications.</p></p>
