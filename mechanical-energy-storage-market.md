<p><p>Mechanical energy storage includes various types such as Pumped Hydro Storage (PHS), Compressed Air Energy Storage (CAES), and others. PHS involves pumping water to a higher reservoir when energy is abundant, and releasing it to generate electricity during peak demand. CAES stores energy by compressing air in underground caverns and releasing it to generate power. Other types of mechanical energy storage technologies include flywheels, which store kinetic energy, and thermal energy storage systems, which store energy in the form of heat.</p></p>
