<p><p>Rapid thermal annealing equipment comes in two main types: lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based systems use powerful lamps to quickly heat the sample to high temperatures, while laser-based systems use lasers for precise and localized heating. Lamp-based systems are more common and cost-effective, making them suitable for high-throughput applications. On the other hand, laser-based systems offer better temperature control and uniformity, making them ideal for research and development purposes where precision is crucial. Both types play a vital role in various industries requiring rapid thermal processing.</p></p>
