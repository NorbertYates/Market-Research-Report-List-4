<p><p>Outdoor spotlights come in three main market types: recessed, surface mounted, and pendant. Recessed spotlights are installed flush with the surface, providing a seamless and unobtrusive appearance. Surface mounted spotlights are attached directly onto the surface, offering a more traditional and easily adjustable option. Pendant spotlights hang from above, creating a stylish and contemporary lighting solution perfect for outdoor settings. Each type offers its own unique advantages and aesthetics, catering to different design preferences and functional requirements.</p></p>
