<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inductive Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental</li><li>Absolute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive encoders are used in various industries for precise position sensing. Incremental encoders provide relative position information by counting pulses as the shaft rotates. Absolute encoders provide exact position information through a unique code for each position. Incremental encoders are cost-effective and require a reference point for accurate reading, while absolute encoders offer accurate positioning without needing a reference point. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
