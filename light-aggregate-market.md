<p><ul><li>Natural Lightweight Aggregate</li><li>Artificial Lightweight Aggregate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lightweight aggregate market can be divided into two main types: natural lightweight aggregate and artificial lightweight aggregate. Natural lightweight aggregate is made from naturally occurring materials such as pumice, volcanic rock, or clay. Artificial lightweight aggregate is produced by heating materials like fly ash or slag to create lightweight aggregates. Both types of aggregates are used in construction for their low density and ability to reduce the overall weight of concrete structures, making them easier to transport and handle.</p></p>
