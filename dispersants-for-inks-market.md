<p><p>Dispersants for inks are essential in maintaining stability and consistency in ink formulations. Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) dispersants are widely used for their high strength and durability in suspending pigments. Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose (HPMC) dispersants are known for their excellent water retention properties, making them ideal for water-based inks. Other dispersants, such as acrylic polymers and cellulose ethers, provide a range of benefits including improved flow properties, adhesion, and color dispersion. Each type of dispersant plays a crucial role in enhancing the performance of inks for various applications.</p></p>
