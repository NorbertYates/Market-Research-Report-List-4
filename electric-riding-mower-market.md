<p><p>The electric riding mower market is broadly categorized into four types: Rotary Mower, Reel Mower, Sickle Mower, and Others. Rotary mowers have blades that rotate horizontally and are ideal for cutting thick grass and weeds. Reel mowers have a cylindrical blade that rotates vertically and provide a clean cut, making them suitable for fine grass. Sickle mowers have a reciprocating blade for cutting rough and uneven terrains. Other types of electric riding mowers include robotic mowers and hover mowers, offering alternative solutions for lawn maintenance.</p></p>
