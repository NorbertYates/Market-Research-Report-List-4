<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam Insulation market consists of two main types: Two component open-cell foam (ocSPF) and Two component closed-cell foam (ccSPF). ocSPF is known for its flexibility and ability to expand to fill spaces, making it ideal for indoor applications. ccSPF, on the other hand, is denser and provides a higher R-value, making it better suited for outdoor use and areas requiring increased insulation. Both types offer energy efficiency and help reduce air infiltration, making them popular choices in the construction industry.</p></p>
