<p><p>Dairy starter cultures are essential components in the production of various dairy products. The market can be categorized into three main types: mesophilic, thermophilic, and probiotics. Mesophilic cultures are used for products like cheddar and brie cheese, while thermophilic cultures are used for yogurt and some Italian cheeses. Probiotics are live microorganisms added to products like yogurt and fermented milk to provide health benefits. Each type of culture plays a specific role in the fermentation process and contributes to the characteristics of the final product.</p></p>
