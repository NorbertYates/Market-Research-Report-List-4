<p><p>Pool alarms are available in various types to enhance pool safety. Fence/Wall Mounted Pool Alarms are installed on the pool perimeter to alert when someone tries to access the pool area. Pool Mounted Infrared Detectors use beams to detect motion near the pool. Underwater Motion Alarms detect movement in the water and can prevent accidents. Other pool alarms include wearable devices or smartphone apps that notify users of unauthorized pool entry. Each type of alarm serves the common purpose of preventing drowning incidents in pools.</p></p>
