<p><p>Contrast injectors are devices used to introduce contrast dye into the body for imaging procedures. There are three main market types for contrast injectors based on administration route: orally, rectally, and intravenously. Orally administered contrast injectors are typically used for gastrointestinal imaging. Rectal contrast injectors are used for imaging the lower gastrointestinal tract. Intravenous contrast injectors are used for a wide range of diagnostic imaging procedures, including CT scans and MRIs. Each market type serves a specific purpose in providing clear and detailed images for medical professionals.</p></p>
