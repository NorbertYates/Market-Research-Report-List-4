<p><p>Specialty fertilizers market is categorized into different types based on their primary nutrient content. Nitrogenous fertilizers provide plants with nitrogen, crucial for overall growth and productivity. Other specialty fertilizers include micronutrient fertilizers and bio-stimulant fertilizers, which enhance plant health and performance through the supply of essential trace elements and organic compounds. Each type plays a specific role in promoting plant growth and health.</p></p>
