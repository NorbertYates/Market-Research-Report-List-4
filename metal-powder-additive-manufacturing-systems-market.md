<p><p>Metal powder additive manufacturing systems market types include Selective Laser Melting (SLM), Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and others. SLM uses a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powders to create 3D objects. EBM utilizes an electron beam to melt metal powder layer by layer, producing high-density parts with complex geometries. Other types of systems may include binder jetting or directed energy deposition methods. Each method offers unique advantages and applications in the additive manufacturing industry.</p></p>
