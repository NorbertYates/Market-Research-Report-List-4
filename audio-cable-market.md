<p><ul><li>Analog Audio Cable</li><li>Digital Audio Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio cable market can be divided into two main types: analog audio cables and digital audio cables. Analog audio cables transmit electrical signals in a continuous wave form, making them suitable for traditional audio devices like record players and older stereo systems. On the other hand, digital audio cables transmit data in binary form, making them ideal for modern digital devices like smartphones, computers, and high-definition audio equipment. Both types of cables serve different purposes and cater to different types of audio equipment.</p></p>
