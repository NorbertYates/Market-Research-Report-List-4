<p><strong>The Tool Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Screw-on Type</li><li>Counter-bore Weld Type</li><li>Flash or Inertia Weld Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tool joints are components used in drilling equipment to connect drill pipes. There are three main types of tool joint market: threaded screw-on type, counter-bore weld type, and flash or inertia weld type. Threaded screw-on type tool joints are attached by screwing onto the drill pipe. Counter-bore weld type tool joints are welded onto the drill pipe by removing a section of the pipe and welding the tool joint in its place. Flash or inertia weld type tool joints are welded onto the drill pipe using a high-energy welding process. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements.</p></p>
