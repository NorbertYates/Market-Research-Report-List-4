<p><p>The Industrial IoT Chips market can be segmented into five types: Processor, Sensor, Connectivity IC, Memory Device, and Logic Device. Processors are responsible for carrying out calculations and decision-making in IoT devices. Sensors collect data from the environment. Connectivity ICs enable communication between devices. Memory Devices store data and instructions. Logic Devices control the flow of data within the device. Each of these types plays a critical role in powering and connecting IoT devices in industrial environments.</p></p>
