<p><p>Rotary Steerable Systems are advanced drilling technologies used in the oil and gas industry. Push the Bit systems have a push force mechanism at the drill bit that assists in steering the drill. Point the Bit systems utilize downhole tools to orient the drill bit in the desired direction. Both systems offer precise steering capabilities while drilling horizontal and directional wells, increasing efficiency and reducing drilling time. These technologies have revolutionized the drilling process, making it more accurate and cost-effective.</p></p>
