<p><p>LED Inspection Lights can be categorized into two types based on their mounting capabilities: Magnetic and Non-Magnetic. Magnetic Inspection Lights come equipped with a strong magnet that allows them to be easily attached to metal surfaces, providing hands-free illumination in tight spaces. On the other hand, Non-Magnetic Inspection Lights do not have a magnet and are typically used in situations where magnetic interference is a concern. Both types offer efficient and reliable lighting solutions for inspection tasks in various industries.</p></p>
