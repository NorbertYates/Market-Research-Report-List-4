<p><p>Soil amendment market is divided into three main types: powder, granular, and liquid soil amendments. Powder soil amendments are finely ground materials that are easily mixed into the soil. Granular soil amendments are small particles that are spread over the soil surface and gradually break down over time. Liquid soil amendments are solutions that are directly applied to the soil for quick absorption. Each type of soil amendment offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the soil and plants.</p></p>
