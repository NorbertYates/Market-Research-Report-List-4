<p><p>Vacuum impregnation equipment market can be divided into two types based on the process used: wet and dry impregnation. In the wet impregnation process, the materials are filled with a liquid solution under vacuum conditions, allowing the solution to penetrate the pores of the material. On the other hand, dry impregnation involves filling the materials with a dry powder or solid material under vacuum, ensuring the material is completely coated. Both wet and dry impregnation methods are used in various industries to enhance the properties of materials.</p></p>
