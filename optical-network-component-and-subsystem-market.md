<p><p>Active components in the optical network market refer to devices that require an external power source to operate, such as amplifiers, switches, and transmitters. Passive components do not require a power source and include products like optical filters, couplers, and splitters. The optical modules market includes components like optical transceivers and transponders that convert electrical signals to optical signals for transmission over fiber networks. These different types of components and subsystems play a key role in ensuring efficient and reliable data transmission within optical networks.</p></p>
