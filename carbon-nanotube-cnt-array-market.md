<p><p>Carbon Nanotube (CNT) arrays can be classified into two main types: single-walled and multi-walled. Single-walled CNT arrays consist of a single cylindrical tube of carbon atoms, while multi-walled CNT arrays contain multiple concentric tubes. Both types have unique properties and applications in various industries such as electronics, energy storage, and aerospace. Single-walled CNT arrays are known for their exceptional strength and conductivity, while multi-walled CNT arrays offer improved thermal and mechanical properties. These materials have a wide range of potential uses and continue to be actively researched and developed.</p></p>
