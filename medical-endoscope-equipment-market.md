<p><p>Medical endoscope equipment market is divided into two main types: hard tube endoscopes and hose endoscopes. Hard tube endoscopes are rigid and have a fixed shape, making them suitable for more precise and targeted procedures. On the other hand, hose endoscopes are flexible and can bend around corners, allowing for a wider range of motion during procedures. Both types of endoscopes are essential in various medical specialties such as gastroenterology, urology, and gynecology for diagnosing and treating patients.</p></p>
