<p><p>Marine LNG fuel tanks come in three main types: Type A tanks, which are prismatic tanks of atmospheric type; Type B tanks, which can be prismatic or spherical in shape; and Type C tanks, which are insulated cylindrical tanks. Type A tanks are designed to operate at atmospheric pressure, while Type B tanks can withstand higher pressures. Type C tanks are cylindrical in shape and are insulated to maintain low temperatures. Each type of tank has its own advantages and applications in the marine LNG fuel market.</p></p>
