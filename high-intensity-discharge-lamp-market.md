<p><p>High-intensity discharge lamp market includes various types such as gas discharge lamp, high-pressure gas discharge lamp, and electrodeless discharge lamp. Gas discharge lamps rely on gas such as mercury or sodium to produce light when an electrical current passes through. High-pressure gas discharge lamps operate at a higher pressure than regular gas discharge lamps, producing a brighter light. Electrodeless discharge lamps use microwaves to excite gas molecules, creating light without the need for electrodes. Each type offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
