<p><p>Aromatherapy products can be categorized into three main market types based on their usage: Topical Application, Aerial Diffusion, and Direct Inhalation. Topical Application involves applying essential oils directly to the skin, either through massage or through lotions and creams. Aerial Diffusion involves dispersing essential oils into the air using diffusers or sprays. Direct Inhalation involves inhaling essential oils either directly from the bottle or through steam inhalation. Each market type offers unique benefits and applications for aromatherapy enthusiasts.</p></p>
