<p><ul><li>Solid Shaft</li><li>Hollow Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary feedthrough market types include solid shaft and hollow shaft feedthroughs. Solid shaft feedthroughs have a single, continuous shaft that rotates to allow the transfer of fluids or gases in vacuum systems. On the other hand, hollow shaft feedthroughs have a hollow core where the material can pass through. Both types are commonly used in industries where precise control and reliability are needed for transferring materials in vacuum environments.</p></p>
