<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fixed RFID Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID System</li><li>HF RFID System</li><li>UHF RFID System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed RFID systems are classified into different market types based on the frequency at which they operate. LF (Low Frequency) RFID systems typically operate around 125-134 kHz and are commonly used for access control and animal tracking. HF (High Frequency) RFID systems operate around 13.56 MHz and are often used for payment transactions and ticketing. UHF (Ultra High Frequency) RFID systems operate around 860-960 MHz and are used for inventory management and supply chain applications. Other market types include Microwave RFID systems and Active RFID systems.</p></p>
