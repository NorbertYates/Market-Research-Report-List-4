<p><p>Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) compressors come in different types to suit various applications in the industry. Vertically-split barrel compressors are compact and efficient, making them suitable for offshore installations. Horizontally-split compressors are commonly used in LNG plants and offer easier maintenance access. Axial compressors provide high flow rates and are ideal for large-scale LNG production facilities. Each type of compressor caters to specific needs in the LNG industry, ensuring reliable and efficient operation of LNG facilities.</p></p>

<p><p>Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) compressors are used in both medium and low-pressure applications to transport LNG efficiently. Medium-pressure compressors are typically used in liquefaction plants and storage facilities, where they help maintain the required pressure levels for LNG storage and distribution. Low-pressure compressors, on the other hand, are used in regasification terminals to convert LNG back into its gaseous form for distribution. Both types of compressors play a crucial role in the LNG supply chain by ensuring safe and reliable transportation of LNG.</p></p>
