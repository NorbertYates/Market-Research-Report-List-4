<p><strong>The Flexible Waterproofing Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-Component</li><li>Two-Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible waterproofing coatings are categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component. One-component coatings come ready to use, eliminating the need for mixing before application. They are easy to apply and are suitable for smaller projects. On the other hand, two-component coatings require mixing of two separate components before application. These coatings offer greater flexibility and durability, making them ideal for larger projects or areas that are subject to heavier wear and tear. Both types of coatings provide effective waterproofing solutions for a variety of surfaces.</p></p>
