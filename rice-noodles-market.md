<p><p>Rice noodles are available in three main market types: fresh, dried, and instant. Fresh rice noodles are soft and have a chewy texture, commonly used in stir-fries and soups. Dried rice noodles are thin and fragile, requiring soaking before use, ideal for dishes like pad thai. Instant rice noodles are precooked and only need to be soaked in hot water for a few minutes before consuming, making them a convenient option for quick meals.
