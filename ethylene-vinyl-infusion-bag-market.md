<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Infusion Bags are commonly available in two market types: Single Chamber and Multi-Chamber. </p><p>Single Chamber bags consist of a single compartment for storing fluids, making them ideal for simple infusion therapies. On the other hand, Multi-Chamber bags have multiple compartments for storing different types of fluids or medications separately, which can be mixed when needed. This allows for more complex infusion treatments to be administered effectively. Both types of bags provide healthcare professionals with options for delivering various medications and fluids to patients efficiently.</p></p>
