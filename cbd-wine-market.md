<p><p>CBD wine market types can be divided into two categories: hemp-based CBD wine and marijuana-based wine. Hemp-based CBD wine is made using extracts from hemp plants, which contain high levels of cannabidiol (CBD) but very low levels of THC, the psychoactive compound in marijuana. On the other hand, marijuana-based wine is made using extracts from marijuana plants, which contain higher levels of THC. Both types of CBD wine offer various health and wellness benefits, but hemp-based CBD wine is more widely available and legal in more regions.</p></p>
