<p><strong>The Disposable Infusion Sets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump Infusion Set</li><li>Non-pump Infusion Set</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable infusion sets market includes two main types: pump infusion sets and non-pump infusion sets. Pump infusion sets are designed for use with infusion pumps and provide a continuous flow of medication or fluids into the patient's bloodstream. Non-pump infusion sets, on the other hand, are manually operated and do not require a pump. They are typically used for intermittent or bolus infusion therapies. Both types of infusion sets play a crucial role in delivering accurate and safe doses of medication to patients in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
