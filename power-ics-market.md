<p><ul><li>Power Management IC (PMIC)</li><li>Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power ICs Market Types include Power Management IC (PMIC) and Driver IC. Power Management ICs are used to manage power requirements in various electronic devices such as smartphones, laptops, and tablets. They optimize power consumption and regulate voltage levels to ensure efficient operation. Driver ICs, on the other hand, control the power supplied to components like LEDs, motors, and displays. They ensure that the right amount of power is delivered to these components for proper functionality and performance. Both types play a crucial role in the power management and efficiency of electronic devices.</p></p>
