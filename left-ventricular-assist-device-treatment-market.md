<p><p>Left Ventricular Assist Device Treatment Market Types include Destination Therapy, Bridge to Transplantation, Bridge to Recovery, and Bridge to Destination. Destination Therapy involves using the device as a long-term solution for patients with end-stage heart failure who are not eligible for heart transplantation. Bridge to Transplantation helps stabilize patients while they wait for a suitable donor heart. Bridge to Recovery aims to support patients with acute heart failure until their heart function improves. Bridge to Destination is for patients who are not candidates for heart transplantation but may be eligible in the future.</p></p>
