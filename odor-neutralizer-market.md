<p><p>Odor Neutralizer market consists of two main types: Natural Odor Neutralizers and Synthesis Odor Neutralizers. Natural Odor Neutralizers are made from organic ingredients such as essential oils, plant extracts, and enzymes, which work to neutralize odors without harsh chemicals. On the other hand, Synthesis Odor Neutralizers are made from artificial chemicals that are designed to mimic the odor-neutralizing properties of natural ingredients. Both types of odor neutralizers aim to eliminate unwanted odors effectively, but they differ in their composition and production methods.</p></p>
