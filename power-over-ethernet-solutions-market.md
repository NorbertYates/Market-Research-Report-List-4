<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) solutions market consists of two main types of products: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controllers & ICs, and Powered Device (PD) Controllers & ICs. The PSE Controllers & ICs are used in devices that supply power over Ethernet cables to other devices, while PD Controllers & ICs are used in devices that receive power from Ethernet cables. Both types of products play a crucial role in enabling the deployment of PoE technology in various industries such as telecommunications, healthcare, and industrial automation.</p></p>
