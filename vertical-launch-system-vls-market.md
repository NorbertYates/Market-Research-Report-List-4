<p><ul><li>Hot Launch</li><li>Cold Launch</li><li>Concentric Canister Launch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vertical Launch System (VLS) market consists of three main types: Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. Hot Launch systems ignite the missile inside the canister before it is ejected, allowing for faster reaction times. Cold Launch systems eject the missile first before igniting it, reducing the risk of damage to the launch platform. Concentric Canister Launch systems use a series of concentric canisters to prevent missile exhaust from affecting nearby missiles, enhancing safety and efficiency. Each type offers unique advantages for different applications in the market.</p></p>
