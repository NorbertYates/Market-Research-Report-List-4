<p><strong>The Metal O-Rings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Pressure Non-vented</li><li>External Pressure Vented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal O-Rings are commonly used in various industries for sealing applications. Internal Pressure Non-vented O-Rings are designed to withstand pressure from within the sealed component without venting excess pressure. On the other hand, External Pressure Vented O-Rings are built to handle pressure from the outside while allowing any excess pressure to be released. Both types cater to different sealing requirements based on the direction of pressure exerted on the seal, ensuring effective and efficient sealing solutions for different applications.</p></p>
