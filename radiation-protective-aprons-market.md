<p><p>Radiation protective aprons are available in two main market types: one-piece and two-piece. One-piece aprons are designed as a single garment, providing full-body protection against radiation exposure. On the other hand, two-piece aprons consist of separate top and bottom pieces that can be worn individually or together for added protection. Both types offer varying levels of shielding based on the thickness of the apron material and are commonly used in medical settings to safeguard healthcare professionals from harmful radiation during diagnostic imaging procedures.</p></p>
