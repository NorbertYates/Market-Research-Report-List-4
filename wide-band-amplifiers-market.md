Driver Amplifiers: These amplifiers are designed to provide the necessary gain to drive a signal through a system without distortion, typically used in RF applications.</p><p>2. Power Amplifiers: These amplifiers are used to increase the power of a signal, making them suitable for applications that require high-output power, such as in broadcasting or telecommunications.</p><p>3. Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs): These amplifiers are designed to amplify weak signals while introducing minimal noise, making them ideal for applications where signal integrity is crucial, such as in satellite communications.</p></p>
