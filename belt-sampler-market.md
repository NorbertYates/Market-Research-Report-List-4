<p><p>Belt sampler market includes three main types: Rotary Sweep Sampler, Conventional Cross Stream Sampler, and Others. Rotary Sweep Sampler is designed for accurate and reliable collection of material from a moving conveyor belt. Conventional Cross Stream Sampler takes a smaller cross-sectional sample from a moving stream of material on a conveyor belt. Other types of belt samplers may include spot samplers, composite samplers, and increment samplers, which offer different methods of collecting and analyzing material from conveyor belts.</p></p>
