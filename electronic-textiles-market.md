<p><p>Passive electronic textiles are fabrics that can conduct electricity but do not require an external power source. Active electronic textiles, on the other hand, have components such as sensors or LEDs that need a power source to function. Ultra-electronic textiles are advanced fabrics that integrate cutting-edge technology like flexible batteries or microprocessors. These three types of electronic textiles cater to different needs and applications, ranging from simple conductivity to complex computing capabilities, expanding the possibilities of smart and functional textiles in various industries.</p></p>
