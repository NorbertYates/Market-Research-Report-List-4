Cellular systems use existing mobile networks to transmit data, offering real-time monitoring and alerts. Satellite systems, on the other hand, use satellite signals to provide coverage in areas where cellular networks are unavailable. This enables vehicles to be tracked in even the most isolated locations. Both types of telematics systems are essential for improving efficiency, safety, and maintenance of off-highway vehicles.</p></p>
