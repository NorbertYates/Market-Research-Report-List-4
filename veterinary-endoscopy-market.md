<p><p>Veterinary endoscopy market can be divided into two main types: flexible endoscopy and rigid endoscopy. Flexible endoscopy involves using a flexible tube with a light and camera attached to examine internal organs and tissues. On the other hand, rigid endoscopy uses a straight and inflexible tube to perform similar procedures. Both types of endoscopy offer veterinarians a minimally invasive way to diagnose and treat various medical conditions in animals, leading to quicker recovery times and better outcomes.</p></p>
