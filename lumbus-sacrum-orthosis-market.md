<p><p>Lumbosacral orthosis, commonly known as LS brace, is divided into two main types - soft brace and rigid brace. Soft braces are made of flexible materials like neoprene and are used for mild to moderate back pain. They provide support and compression without restricting movement. On the other hand, rigid braces are made of hard plastics or metals and are used for more severe spinal conditions or post-surgery support. They immobilize the spine to aid in healing and stability. Both types cater to different needs in the lumbosacral orthosis market.</p></p>
