<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Traffic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Loop</li><li>Piezoelectric Sensor</li><li>Bending Plate</li><li>Image Sensor</li><li>Infrared Sensor</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive Loop sensors use electromagnetic fields to detect vehicles passing over the loop. Piezoelectric sensors generate electric charge when mechanical stress is applied, detecting vehicles based on vibrations. Bending plate sensors measure the pressure applied when vehicles pass over. Image sensors use cameras to visually detect vehicles. Infrared sensors use infrared light to detect vehicles passing by. Other types of sensors in the market may include ultrasonic sensors, microwave sensors, and radar sensors, each utilizing different technologies to detect traffic flow.</p></p>
