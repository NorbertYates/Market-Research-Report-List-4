<p><p>The paper mill rolls market is segmented into various types based on their functions and applications in the paper manufacturing process. Press rolls are used to squeeze excess water out of the paper pulp. Wire rolls help in forming the paper sheet. Felt rolls assist in drying the paper sheet. Couch rolls transfer the paper sheet from the wire to the drying section. Breast rolls guide the paper sheet along the machine. Calendaring rolls smoothen and polish the paper. Suction rolls remove water from the paper sheet. Other rolls include embossing, reel, and spreader rolls.</p></p>
